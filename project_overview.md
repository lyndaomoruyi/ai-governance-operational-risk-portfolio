# Project Overview

## Portfolio Title
AI Governance & Operational Risk Automation Portfolio

## Flagship Project
Inventory Risk, Client Compliance & Replenishment Automation System

## Business Problem
Venue-based commercial operations can experience stockout risk when product levels, reorder thresholds, vendor follow-ups, documentation evidence, and client reporting are reviewed manually across multiple entertainment accounts. Manual review can delay replenishment decisions, increase missed follow-ups, and reduce stakeholder visibility.

## Solution
This project models an AI-powered automation workflow that reviews synthetic operational data, classifies risks, generates action items, maps compliance controls, creates stakeholder summaries, and supports project delivery artifacts.

## What AI Automates
- Identifies stockout, documentation, reporting, vendor, and escalation risks
- Classifies each item into Low, Medium, or High risk
- Generates recommended follow-up actions
- Converts operational issues into backlog items and acceptance criteria
- Produces leadership-ready summary language
- Supports UAT validation and human review checkpoints

## Business Value
The system demonstrates how AI can reduce manual review, improve operational risk visibility, strengthen client compliance tracking, and support faster decision-making.
