# AI Governance & Third-Party Risk Compliance Portfolio

## Flagship Project
**AI Use Case Inventory, Vendor Risk & Compliance Control Monitoring System**

## Portfolio Purpose
This independent banking compliance portfolio uses synthetic data to demonstrate how a regulated financial institution could inventory AI use cases, review third-party/vendor risk, monitor compliance controls, track missing evidence, and preserve audit-ready documentation.

## Business Problem
Banks and regulated organizations are adopting AI-enabled tools across customer service, compliance monitoring, fraud operations, audit support, vendor due diligence, lending support, document review, and internal workflow automation. Without a structured inventory and compliance review workflow, teams can lose visibility into:

- AI tools operating without approved use-case documentation
- vendor-hosted tools with incomplete due diligence
- customer data exposure and privacy risks
- missing model cards, SOC reports, or data-flow documentation
- limited human oversight for high-impact use cases
- overdue compliance reviews and unresolved remediation items

## Solution
The project models an AI governance and compliance monitoring workflow that captures AI use cases, assigns risk scores, flags missing evidence, maps controls, tracks open issues, and supports human-reviewed compliance decisions.

## Evidence Built
- AI use-case inventory workbook
- synthetic AI/vendor risk dataset
- dashboard summary
- vendor risk review tracker
- compliance control matrix
- issue/action log
- UAT test cases
- data dictionary and sources
- employer-facing website page
