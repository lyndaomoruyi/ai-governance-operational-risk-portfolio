Business Problem

Manual inventory and compliance follow-up creates operational risk.

Commercial operations teams can lose visibility when product availability, reorder thresholds, vendor follow-ups, documentation evidence, and client reporting are tracked manually across multiple entertainment accounts.

This project models a stronger AI-enabled workflow that flags risk earlier, generates actions, and gives business and compliance stakeholders a clearer view of what needs attention.

Manual Work Replaced

  • Reviewing inventory rows one by one
  • Manually identifying stockout risk
  • Drafting status updates from scratch
  • Creating follow-up tasks manually
  • Tracking documentation gaps separately
  • Preparing leadership summaries manually
