Project Overview

From manual inventory review to AI-supported risk automation

This project models a business environment where entertainment-account inventory, client reporting expectations, documentation status, vendor follow-up, and replenishment actions must be monitored consistently. The portfolio demonstrates how AI can reduce manual review by classifying risk, producing action items, and supporting validated reporting workflows.

Business Problem

Manual tracking can delay visibility into low inventory, overdue follow-ups, missing documentation, and stakeholder reporting needs.

AI Solution

AI-assisted workflows classify risk, identify escalation needs, generate action items, and create leadership-ready summaries for human review.

Business Value

The portfolio demonstrates compliance visibility, operational accountability, project delivery structure, and responsible AI validation.

AI Automation Workflow

How the system reduces manual effort

The workflow is designed to show how AI can assist with classification, documentation, task generation, and reporting while keeping human validation in place.

1

Ingest Data

Review synthetic inventory, account, vendor, documentation, and follow-up records.

2

Classify Risk

Flag stockout, replenishment, reporting, documentation, and escalation risks.

3

Map Controls

Connect each risk to control activity, evidence, owner, status, and validation notes.

4

Create Actions

Generate backlog items, remediation tasks, follow-ups, and stakeholder update prompts.

5

Validate Output

Document human review checkpoints, UAT scenarios, pass/fail results, and governance limits.
