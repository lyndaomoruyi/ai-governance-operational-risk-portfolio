# Business Requirements Document

## Project Name
Inventory Risk, Client Compliance & Replenishment Automation System

## Objective
Create an AI-assisted workflow that helps operational and compliance teams identify replenishment risk, documentation gaps, client reporting issues, and overdue follow-up actions before they become service or compliance concerns.

## Stakeholders
- Commercial Operations
- Project Analyst / Business Analyst
- Compliance / Risk Owner
- Vendor Management
- Fulfillment / Inventory Team
- Client-facing Account Stakeholders

## Functional Requirements
1. System shall ingest inventory and operational tracking data.
2. System shall calculate days of cover and compare inventory to reorder thresholds.
3. System shall classify each record by risk type and risk rating.
4. System shall flag missing documentation, overdue vendor follow-ups, and pending client reports.
5. System shall generate recommended action items and escalation notes.
6. System shall maintain owner, due date, status, and evidence fields.
7. System shall provide dashboard views for leadership and stakeholder reporting.
8. System shall support UAT validation through pass/fail test cases.

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Use synthetic data in public portfolio version.
- Keep risk logic explainable.
- Maintain human validation checkpoints.
- Provide downloadable artifacts for recruiter review.
- Publish project through an employer-facing portfolio website.
