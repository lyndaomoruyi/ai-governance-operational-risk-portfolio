Business Requirements Document

Business need and functional requirements

Financial institutions need a practical way to identify, review, monitor, and evidence AI use cases across business areas and vendor-supported workflows.

Stakeholders

  • Compliance
  • Risk Management
  • IT Security
  • Vendor Management
  • Business Owners
  • Internal Audit
  • Legal / Privacy

Core requirements

  • Capture AI use case, owner, vendor, purpose, data type, customer impact, and review status.
  • Assign risk tier using exposure, evidence, oversight, approval, and review timing.
  • Track vendor due diligence, control mapping, open issues, owners, and due dates.
  • Provide a leadership-ready view of high-risk items, missing evidence, and overdue reviews.

Success measures

  • AI use cases are visible in one inventory.
  • High-risk and overdue reviews are easy to identify.
  • Evidence gaps and remediation actions have owners.
  • Controls are mapped to evidence and review cadence.
Risk Logic / Control Design

Risk classification and monitoring logic

The project uses a practical triage model to decide which AI use cases require escalation, enhanced review, monitoring, or routine control validation.

Risk drivers

Data sensitivityRestricted, confidential, customer, or operational data increases review priority.
Customer impactAI use touching customers, communications, decisions, or service outcomes receives heightened review.
Vendor exposureVendor-hosted or embedded AI requires due diligence evidence and subcontractor awareness.
OversightLimited human review, missing approval, or unclear override steps create control gaps.
Review timingOverdue reviews and open remediation actions move items into the stakeholder queue.

Control expectations

InventoryOwner, purpose, business area, AI category, vendor, and data type are captured.Baseline
EvidenceSOC reports, vendor review, data terms, privacy review, and approval status are tracked.Required
OversightHuman review, escalation, override, and monitoring responsibilities are documented.Required
Issue actionGaps are assigned to owners with severity, due date, remediation action, and status.Track
