What This Project Shows

This portfolio demonstrates a practical compliance workflow that can support regulated financial-services review of AI tools and vendor-enabled AI capabilities.

  • AI use-case intake and inventory visibility
  • Vendor and third-party AI risk tracking
  • Evidence management for SOC reports, model cards, due diligence, and oversight documentation
  • Risk scoring based on data sensitivity, customer impact, approval status, and control readiness
  • Issue and remediation tracking for overdue reviews, missing evidence, and human oversight gaps
  • UAT validation and audit-ready documentation
